Civil War

Three scenes from a fractured nation — a peaceful river, a battlefield's smoldering aftermath, and the long road north.

Scene 01

Mississippi River Forest

Daylight along a clear blue river — deer, herons, fish, a steamboat in the distance.
Scene 02

Civil War Battlefield

Dusk after the battle — broken cannons, mud, flickering flames, Grant and Lee on opposite ridges.
Scene 03

Slave Grounds

Moonlit forest, fireflies, the North Star — silhouettes walking the Underground Railroad.
Scene 05

Lake Fortress

Two stone fortresses on rocky islands. Aim cannons (↑/↓), fire (SPACE), send boats (B) — first fortress to fall loses.
